Privacy Policy

Everybody House Games

Last updated: 22 April 2025

1. Overview

Everybody House Games ("we," "us," or "our") publishes mobile games on Google Play. We respect your privacy. Our games do not collect, store, or transmit any personal or non‑personal user data.

2. Data Collection

We do not:

  • collect personal information (e.g., name, email, address, phone number);
  • collect device identifiers or analytics data;
  • access location, contacts, photos, or other device‑stored content;
  • use cookies, SDKs, or third‑party trackers.

3. Data Sharing

Because no data are collected, no data are shared with third parties for any reason (including advertising, analytics, or marketing).

4. Children's Privacy

Our games are suitable for general audiences. As we collect no data, we comply with COPPA and other child‑privacy regulations by default.

5. Permissions

Our apps request no runtime permissions beyond those essential for basic operation as declared in the Google Play manifest (e.g., internet permission solely for downloading game assets). No permission is used to gather data.

6. Security

Since we retain no information, there is no risk of data breach. If this policy changes and data collection begins, we will implement industry‑standard security measures.

7. Changes to This Policy

If future versions of our games require data collection, we will update this policy and notify users through the Google Play listing before such changes take effect.
